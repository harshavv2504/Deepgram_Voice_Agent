# IndiVillage Tech Solutions - Where We Operate

## Geographic Presence

**IndiVillage operates 12 centers** across rural India, with the first center established in **Yemmiganur, India in 2010**.

## Rural Focus

The company strategically locates its operations in rural areas to create employment opportunities and drive economic development in underserved communities.
